Family Size Choropleth Maps

These PUMA- and state-level maps show the percentage of women aged 25-55 who are married and have a certain number of children present in their household.
These maps are based on ACS 1-year estimates for 2022 and 2023; IPUMS was used as the source for both this data and the PUMA shapefiles. (State-level shapefiles came from the US Census.)
